## 1. Comparison with Existing Systems (Section 3)

### Location
- Placed after Related Work (Section 2)
- Before System Architecture (now Section 4)

### Content Overview
This section provides a comprehensive comparison of our system with existing AI-powered interview and assessment platforms.

#### Subsections:

1. **Comparison Framework**
   - Compares systems across 6 key dimensions
   - Includes detailed comparison table (Table: tab:system_comparison)
   - Compares with: Fixed Template Systems, Simple CAT Systems, LLM-Only Systems, Traditional Platforms

2. **Adaptive Difficulty Adjustment**
   - Highlights our 45-50% reduction in convergence time
   - Compares with IRT-based methods (8-12 questions vs. our 4.2 questions)
   - Discusses advantages over LLM-only systems

3. **Question Selection Strategies**
   - Compares multi-priority approach with fixed templates and CAT systems
   - Highlights unique combination of gap targeting, personalization, and coverage

4. **Evaluation Methodology**
   - Compares hybrid framework with rule-based and LLM-only approaches
   - Emphasizes 99.2% availability through automatic fallback

5. **Personalization Capabilities**
   - Discusses resume-based personalization integration
   - Compares fuzzy matching approach with exact matching

6. **System Availability and Reliability**
   - Highlights ability to operate without LLM services
   - Discusses automatic fallback mechanism

7. **Multimodal Support**
   - Compares audio processing capabilities
   - Discusses speech analysis features

8. **Summary of Advantages**
   - Lists 6 key advantages of our system
   - Emphasizes integrated approach
